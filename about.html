About Me

Education

I am a second year Bioinformatics student at Universiti Teknologi Malaysia. Before studying at university, I obtained my STPM certification at Kolej Tingkatan Enam Petaling Jaya, where I majored in the life sciences. I moved around a lot to study, having been to Kelantan, Selangor, Sarawak and now Johor for my education.

Interests

In my free time, I like to pick up new hobbies to see which ones will stick. Currently I am in my phase of doing competitive programming questions. When I'm sick of that, I typically pick up a book or watch Youtube videos. I have a huge passion for science communication and education, so I volunteer in those areas as well from time to time.

Goals

In the future, I would enjoy working in a rigorous and academic setting. I want to use the skills and knowledge that I have picked up throughout my years of education for good and to serve humanity.
